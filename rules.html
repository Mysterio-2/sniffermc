Server Rules

🛡️ Player Conduct

  1. Do not impersonate Staff or players via /nick, name changes, or alts.
  2. Do not ask Staff for money, items, ranks, or OP.
  3. Avoid controversial topics in Global Chat.
  4. No chat spamming or excessive repetition.
  5. Avoid creating TP traps or unsafe TPs.

💬 Communication Guidelines

  1. Global Chat is English-only.
  2. No advertising of other servers or services.
  3. Respect and listen to Staff.
  4. Keep arguments private.
  5. Avoid discussing sensitive personal topics.

🚫 Prohibited Behaviors

  1. Keep chat appropriate and respectful.
  2. Do not share private personal information.
  3. No discussions of self-harm or depression.
  4. Griefing and land destruction are forbidden.
  5. Manage spawner and redstone areas to prevent lag.

🏰 Server Integrity

  1. Maintain distance between builds (250 blocks).
  2. No impersonation via nicknames or disguises.
  3. Play fairly without hacks or exploits.
  4. Avoid using special chat character codes.
  5. Only one alt account per player.
  6. Report bugs; do not exploit them.

🔔 Rules are subject to Staff interpretation. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, or bans.
