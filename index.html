How to Get Started

  1. Pick a Job

    Use /jobs browse to view available jobs. Choose from Miner, Farmer, or Hunter to start earning money.

  2. Explore the Economy

    Visit /shop to buy and sell items. Build your fortune by trading or investing in rare items.

  3. Level Up

    Complete tasks to gain experience and advance through the 100 levels.

  4. Elemental Journey

    Gather elements:
    - Mine for Earth and Fire
    - Farm for Water
    - Battle mobs for Shadow
    - Explore for Light and Air
